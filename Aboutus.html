EAPC GATUKU CDC E-ABOUT US

EAPC Gatuku CDC is a Compassion-assisted project that works to retrieve children from poverty and bring them them up in a Christian way

The compassion has its operations controlled from the main office that is situated in the United States
while the country's operations are run from the country's main office that is mostly situated in one of the big town of the country.
An example with ours that is run by the Kenyan head office that is situated in Karen, Nairobi.

After the main office we have the center's office that now plays the active role of dealing and
working with the children. As with ours, our center's office is situated in Tharaka Nithi county, Tharaka North Sub-county, Mukothima ward, Makutano village
As you approach Makutano market, from the main Gaciongo-Ura major route, shortly after crossing the Thangatha Bridge, you find a signboard on your right side that directs you to our center
The center is located on EAPC GATUKU CHURCH'S premises along Gatuku Elite Academy

As Gatuku Project Center, we also try our best to assist the sorrounding communities by offering some artesian courses that can help them to earn a living.
Those courses include;
  • Carpentry
  • Salon
  • Tailoring
  • Computer Skills
